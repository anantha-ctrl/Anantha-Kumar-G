I'm Anantha Kumar G

Full Stack Developer

I am Anantha Kumar, a passionate and detail-focused web and software developer from Tuticorin, and a recent B.E graduate in Computer Science and Engineering from GRACE College of Engineering, Thoothukudi. I specialize in building responsive and dynamic web applications with a strong foundation in full stack development.

My technical skill set includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Java, JSP, Spring Boot, and MySQL. I've developed impactful projects like "Ask Expert" – a platform that connects users with specialists, and a Task Management System that supports secure and efficient task operations.

I have completed internships as a Full Stack Developer at NoviTech R&D Pvt Ltd and as a Frontend Developer at CODTECH IT SOLUTION. My time at StackNQueue has been especially valuable, providing real-time experience in building production-ready applications and learning key concepts such as token-based authentication, secure API handling, and frontend integration. I am constantly learning and pushing my boundaries, with the short-term goal of becoming a skilled full stack and app developer, and a long-term aspiration to contribute to secure and scalable digital solutions.

I am actively seeking a full-time job where I can apply my technical skills, contribute to innovative projects, and grow as a developer in a challenging, fast-paced environment. I am committed to continuous learning and look forward to joining a team where I can build meaningful software solutions
